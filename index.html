Welcome to Sana Online Quran House

Our online Quran House has the following objectives, to name just a few of them:

  • For individuals who have no access to facilities in their communities, offer the best Quran learning, practice, and recitation platform.
  • The right pronunciation of the Holy Quran's recitation in accordance with tajweed guidelines will be taught to you by our highly skilled and experienced experts.
  • To facilitate the learning and recitation of the Holy Qur'an, we use particular practical approaches. The opportunity to learn to read the Quran using the correct tajweed techniques is provided by SOQH.
  • Female instructors are offered for female students and children. So that they might discover their comfort zone.

SIGNIFICANCE OF HOLY QURAN

“Read! In the name of your Lord, who has created (all that exists)” (Al Quran)

The Holy Quran is the book of Allah revealed to Prophet Muhammad (P.B.U.H) fourteen hundred years ago. Not even a dot has been displaced in this book since then. It is Allah's final and the last book of guidance for mankind. The direction of the true path is set in this book. Mankind needs to understand the message of the Holy Quran correctly. We must follow its message If we want happiness in this world and the hereafter.

OUR MISSION

“The best among you is he who learns the Quran and teaches it” (Bukhari)

We aim to provide quality Quranic and Islamic education online. The main focus is on reciting the Holy Quran with the rules of Tajweed. Tajweed is necessary for reverence and submission in Salah (Namaz). Moreover, Tajweed is one of the etiquettes to read the holy Quran. Online Quran House considers that the basic knowledge of Islam should reach every Muslim. The “online” aspect of the House is an extension of that goal.

HOW IT WORKS

The courses will be delivered online. You will need a reliable internet connection. Moreover, you'll need a Zoom or Skype ID. Any device that is compatible with these programmes may be used. When you choose, our instructors will be available online. Three of the classes are free to try out. You may decide to continue in the course or discontinue it after these lessons. In particular, webcams are not necessary. The only purpose of the screen-sharing feature is to instruct you. The same screen that the teacher is using can be seen by you thanks to it. The monthly tests are another way we'll evaluate your performance. Every January, we organise a competition. On the other hand, you must sign up for the desired course first. Besides, you can ask for our help to find the right course.

COURSES

BASIC QURAN READING

This course is for those who do not even know the alphabets of Arabic language. Moreover, it covers some very basic rules of Tajweed. You will also learn how to spell the Arabic words and phrases.

QURAN WITH TAJWEED

This course is for those who know how to spell the Arabic words. You are also required to learn the basic rules of Tajweed. You will learn to recite the Holy Quran. Moreover, you will be taught how to apply the rules of Tajweed.

QURAN MEMORIZATION

This course is available to those who already know how to recite the Holy Quran with Tajweed. It needs a lot of hard work and dedication. The completion of this course depends on the effort you put in.
